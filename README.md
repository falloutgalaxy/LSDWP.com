# Los Santos Department of Water & Power (LSDWP)

Official public website for the Los Santos Department of Water & Power.

## About

The Los Santos Department of Water & Power (LSDWP) provides reliable electrical and water services throughout Los Santos. Our teams conduct inspections, repairs, infrastructure upgrades, emergency outage response, and government projects to keep homes, businesses, and city facilities operating safely and efficiently.

## Services

* Electrical Infrastructure
* Water Distribution
* Emergency Outage Response
* Infrastructure Repairs
* Building Inspections
* Government Projects
* Preventative Maintenance
